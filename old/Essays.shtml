INTRODUCTION

For certain violations of the Stevens Honor System, the Honor Board may assign a penalty including a Removable Academic Sanction. These academic sanctions are removed from a students transcript either by completing the Ethics Course, or by completing a penalty essay (or multiple penalty essays). The Manual above outlines the requirements and procedures for completing a penalty essay.

OBJECTIVES

    The objective of the penalty essays is to provide students with an opportunity to learn from their violation. As such, the Honor Board works to select the prompt from a list of prompts tailored to specific violations or situations. The Prompt is selected by the entire Honor Board, to ensure the the most fitting and educative experience possible for the Student.

REQUIREMENTS

When a student is assigned a penalty essay, it is required that the essay be submitted and approved by both their essay mentor and the Executive Board of the Honor Board within two months. If that deadline is not met and the student is not granted an extension, the Academic Sanction will not be removed.

For a comprehensive list of requirements, please reference the Essay Manual at the top of this page.
